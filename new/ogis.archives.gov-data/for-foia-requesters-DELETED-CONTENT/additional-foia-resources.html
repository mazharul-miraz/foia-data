Additional FOIA Resources

  • Citizens for Responsibility and Ethics in Washington
    • Measuring Transparency Under the FOIA- CREW/Openthegovernment.org report, Dec 2011

  • Reporters Committee for Freedom of the Press

    • Federal Open Government Guide
    • FOIA letter generator
      Templates for a requester to build a request/appeal letter

  • Collaboration on Government Secrecy
     A non-partisan educational project devoted to openness in government, freedom of information, government transparency, and the study of "government secrecy" in the United States and internationally
  • First Amendment Center
     Serves as a forum for the study and exploration of free-expression issues, including freedom of speech, of the press and of religion, and the rights to assemble and to petition the government
  • FOIAdvocates
     Links to State public record laws

  • FOIA Blog
     A web site for blogging about everything FOIA and government disclosure
  • FOI Advocate Blog
    The official blog of the National Freedom of Information Coalition and the FOI Advocate

  • National Freedom of Information Coalition
     Tracks news, stories that used open records laws, and policy developments

  • Newspaper Association of America
    Writes periodic updates on FOIA policy developments
  • OpenTheGovernment.org
    • Measuring Transparency Under the FOIA- CREW/Openthegovernment.org report, Dec 2011
  • The Radio-Television Digital News Association
     Section on FOIA under "Best Practices" addresses current issues
  • Sunshine in Government Initiative
    • FOIA Statistics
    • FOIA Files
      Searchable collection of over 600 news and other stories that the federal FOIA made possible
  • PBS History Detectives
    A brief primer on what to expect when filing a FOIA request.
  • Bloggers' Legal Guide to FOIA
    Electronic Frontier Foundation-explains the FOIA, gives tips on how to make a request, gives advice on what to expect when making a request, as well as explains the rights of requesters
  • Society of Environmental Journalists
    Policy developments on federal openness and secrecy, including FOIA, as well as access to environmental information.
  • Society of Professional Journalists
    Includes a FOI audit toolkit, and other resources
  • Archive-It FOIA Collection
    Collection of sites that deal with Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests and documents, run by the Internet Archive
  • Government Attic
    Provides electronic copies of interesting Federal Government documents obtained under the Freedom of Information Act
  • Scribd.com
    Collection by FOIA requester groups of hundreds of documents acquired through the FOIA and other public disclosure, or "sunshine," laws
  • National Security Archive at George Washington University
    Publishes declassified documents obtained through the Freedom of Information Act
  • MuckRock
    Provides a service to file FOIA requests and posts released documents with the requests
