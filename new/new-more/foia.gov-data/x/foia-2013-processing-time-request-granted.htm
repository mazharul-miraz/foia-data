Instructions: You can explore the FOIA data that makes up an agency’s annual FOIA report. Search for data from a single agency or compare data from multiple agencies. Just follow these steps:

  1. At the “Select Report” column, click on a category, such as “Requests” or “Administration”  to select the type of report you would like to run. Learn more about these categories in theGlossary.
  2. Use the (+) or (-) signs appearing next to the text box to select the agency/agencies you want to look up, selecting up to four at a time.
  3. Choose the year you would like to see data from.
  4. Click “Create Report.”  Scroll down to see your customized report.

For help interpreting a report, scroll over the icon.
